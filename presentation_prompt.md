# Prompt for AI Presentation Generator

You are an expert presentation designer and pitch deck specialist. Please create a compelling script and slide outline for a **Google Slides presentation** to be submitted to a **Stablecoin Hackathon**.

## Project Overview
**Product Name**: LexFlow Protocol
**Concept**: An Autonomous Legal Agent & Integrated Payment Platform that combines AI (LLM) and Blockchain to automate contract management, execution, and settlement.
**Theme**: "Stablecoins as the Trust Layer for Legal Tech" - Moving contracts from dead text to dynamic, programmable financial instruments.

## Target Audience & Awards
This presentation must specifically appeal to the judges for two major prizes:
1.  **Ethereum Prize (Technical Depth)**: Highlight Smart Contracts, EIP-712 Signatures, and Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZK-SNARKs/Circom).
2.  **SoftBank Prize (AI Innovation)**: Highlight AI Agents (LangGraph), Semantic Analysis, and the "Redline" negotiation bot.

## Core Narrative
Contracts today are broken: disconnected from payments and require manual verification. LexFlow uses **AI** to understand the contract and **Stablecoins (JPYC)** to execute it perfectly.

## Slide Outline

### Slide 1: Title Slide
- **Title**: LexFlow Protocol
- **Subtitle**: The Intelligent Layer for Legal Engineering
- **Tagline**: "From Static Text to Programmable Payments"
- **Visual**: A futuristic fusion of a legal document transforming into a blockchain transaction block.

### Slide 2: The Problem (Friction & Risk)
- **Key Point 1**: Legal obligations and financial settlements are disconnected. (e.g., "Contract signed but payment forgotton")
- **Key Point 2**: Manual verification is slow and error-prone.
- **Key Point 3**: Privacy risks in sharing sensitive lists (like client lists during conflicts checks).
- **Visual**: A graphic showing a broken bridge between a "Contract (PDF)" and a "Bank Transfer," with a frustrated human in the middle.

### Slide 3: The Solution - LexFlow Ecosystem
- **Overview**: A unified platform where AI parses the law and Blockchain enforces it.
- **Key Pillars**:
    1.  **AI Analysis**: Converts PDF to JSON schemas (LangGraph).
    2.  **Smart Execution**: Automated JPYC payments via x402 Gateway (Stablecoin Theme).
    3.  **Zero Trust Privacy**: ZK Proofs for KYC and Conflict Checks.
- **Visual**: A central "Brain" (AI) connecting documents to a chain (Blockchain), enabling smooth data flow.

### Slide 4: [SoftBank Prize Focus] AI Agentic Workflow (LangGraph)
- **Feature**: AI Contract Parser & Redline Agent.
- **How it works**:
    -   Extracts deadlines, amounts, and obligations from PDF.
    -   "Redline Agent" detects risks (e.g., unlimited liability) and auto-drafts counter-proposals.
    -   Not just OCR, but *Semantic Understanding*.
- **Visual**: A flowchart showing "PDF Input" -> "Agent 1 (Parser)" -> "Agent 2 (Risk Assessor)" -> "Agent 3 (Drafter)" -> "Final Schema".

### Slide 5: [Main Theme] x402 Payment Gateway (JPYC Integration)
- **Feature**: Pay-per-Use & Atomic Settlement.
- **Innovation**: Implementing HTTP 402 (Payment Required) protocol.
- **Flow**: API Request -> 402 Error -> MetaMask JPYC Payment -> Transaction Verified -> Service Delivered.
- **Benefit**: Zero bad debt risk. Global access without credit cards.
- **Visual**: A sequence diagram showing a User paying JPYC to unlock an AI service instantly.

### Slide 6: [Ethereum Prize Focus] Trust & Privacy (ZK & Signatures)
- **Tech Stack**: EIP-712 Typed Signatures + Groth16 ZK-SNARKs.
- **Feature 1 (Signatures)**: Immutable version control. "Who signed what" is mathematically proven on-chain.
- **Feature 2 (ZK Proofs)**:
    -   **KYC**: Prove you are verified without showing ID.
    -   **Conflict of Interest (COI)**: Prove a new client isn't on your secret blocklist without revealing the list.
- **Visual**: A shield icon representing ZK Privacy protecting a user's data while a green checkmark appears on the server side.

### Slide 7: Business Impact & Roadmap
- **Use Cases**:
    -   Startup Fundraising (SAFE + Stablecoin transfer).
    -   Supply Chain (Delivery = Auto-payment).
    -   Legal Tech (Private Conflict Checks).
- **Future**: "Legal Playbooks" where AI learns a firm's negotiation style.

### Slide 8: Conclusion
- **Message**: LexFlow doesn't just manage contracts; it *executes* them. The future of law is programmable code and stable money.
- **Call to Action**: "Join the Legal Engineering Revolution."

​テーマ
「Secured Financeの特性を活かした、RWA／セキュリティトークン（ST）× JPYCによるオンチェーン金融ユースケース」

​選定基準

​技術活用度（Secured Financeらしさ） 担保金融・金利設計（固定金利/固定満期、イールドカーブ等）の考え方が効果的に組み込まれているか

​実現性・イメージの明瞭さ 利用シーンやフローが想像でき、実装を見据えた提案になっているか プロトタイプ/モックがあれば加点対象

​求めるアイデア：
RWA（Real World Asset：不動産・債権等の実物資産）やセキュリティトークン（ST）とJPYCを組み合わせた、オンチェーン金融の新しい体験やビジネスモデルを募集します。

​例：

​RWA/STの流動化：セキュリティトークンを担保にJPYCで資金調達する設計

​TradFi × DeFi / Private × Public：プライベート環境の資産をパブリックチェーンの流動性へ安全に接続する仕組み

​JPYC建ての運用：オンチェーン資産をオフランプ（法定通貨に戻す）せずに効率運用するアイデア（JPYCを調達してUSDC等で高金利運用するキャリー取引、ヘッジ設計込み）
